Generation III - Hoenn

Generation III arrived in 2002 (Japan) and 2003 (worldwide), introducing Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald. This generation brought a major graphical upgrade and new gameplay systems.

Key Features

  • Region: Hoenn
  • Total Pokémon: 135 new, total 386
  • Games: Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, LeafGreen
  • Introduced Pokémon abilities and natures
  • Double battles and Pokémon contests
  • Secret bases and weather-based gameplay
  • Legendary Pokémon: Groudon, Kyogre, Rayquaza, Regice, Regirock, Registeel, Deoxys, Jirachi
